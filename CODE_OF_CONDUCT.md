# Code of Conduct

We want Document Intelligence to be a useful, welcoming project for people of every background
and experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while making impact and safety concerns explicit.
- Protect private document content, credentials, and reporter identities.
- Help newcomers understand the reasoning behind a requested change.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate disclosure of
private data, and sustained disruptive behavior are not accepted in project spaces.

## Reporting and enforcement

Use GitHub's private reporting or moderation tools when possible. Security vulnerabilities follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not a public issue. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close or
lock interactions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior puts the
community or its users at risk.

Reports will be reviewed as privately and promptly as the available information allows. Good-faith
reports will not result in retaliation.

This policy is informed by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.
